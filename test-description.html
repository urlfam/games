How to Play Card Solitaire: Word Game

Core Game Rules

  • Your mission: Sort all cards into the correct categories
  • Key mechanic: Match image cards with word cards by spotting connections
  • Win condition: Place every card correctly before running out of moves
  • Special feature: Limited moves force strategic thinking

Step-by-Step Guide

  1. Study the cards: Look at both image and word cards to understand what you're working with
  2. Find connections: Match pictures with their corresponding words or categories
  3. Plan ahead: Think about move order since you have limited attempts
  4. Complete the level: Sort all cards correctly to advance

Controls Guide

This game features simple tap controls:

  • Mouse click or tap: Select and place cards
  • Drag: Move cards to different positions
  • Hint button: Get help when stuck
